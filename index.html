About

Paran, as known as Rz

A Mario Kart Wii and also an amateur osu! player,
Born on November 5th

In 2010, I started playing Mario Kart Wii with Wii Wheel.

In 2012 or 2013, I started hacking MKWii with the fanmade mod, Darky Kart Wii.
Also (maybe) same year, I've tried to play osu! for the first time.
But I have no brain to understand the game systems, so I temporalily left it away.

In 2015, I finally started creating custom tracks.
I tried making any my own CTs with using the opinion of CTs other people made and tutorials.
And I've finally finished my first released custom track, Cityside Nature in December 18th 2015.

3 years later, I has been resumed playing osu!.
